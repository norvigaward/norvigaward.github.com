Our mission: Promote the study of the web using web-scale technology

Sara and Common Crawl share the vision that to study the web should be possible for everybody. This is based on the believe that data, tools, and knowledge needed for this purpose should be open. As a part of this vision, the Norvig Best Web Study Award aims to promote this knowledge, these tools, and the data.

The deadline for the 2012-2013 Norvig Best Web Study Award is 11:59pm, January 15, 2013. The award is open for applications starting November 15 2012. Make sure to carefully read all the instructions below, including the entry criteria and proposal format.
