Can I apply for this award?

Are you a student or researcher studying at or employed by a research institute or university in the Netherlands? Then you’re eligible to apply!

What should I do?

Run experiments on the Common Crawl dataset using SARA’s Hadoop infrastructure, and demonstrate what is innovative about those experiments.

What can I win?

The winner gets a tablet (type tba), and 1500 Euro to spend on travel, accommodation, and conference registration fee for SIGIR 2013, for one person, to be held in Dublin, Ireland.

Who’s on the jury?

Peter Norvig, Hilary Mason, Jimmy Lin, and Evert Lammerts. Who they are? Find out here.
