Can I apply for this award?

Are you a student or researcher studying at or employed by an university, hogeschool or research institute in the Benelux? Then you can apply! Other residents of the Benelux can also apply, but won't be eligible for the prizes.

What should I do?

Run experiments on the Common Crawl dataset using SURFsara’s Hadoop infrastructure, and demonstrate what is innovative about those experiments.

What can I win?

The winning team gets a tablet, a smart watch and for all members a year-long Github small plan.

Who’s on the jury?

Peter Norvig (Google), Jimmy Lin (University of Maryland), Arjen P. de Vries (CWI) and Evert Lammerts (Lucipher). Who they are? Find out here.
