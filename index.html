The 2014 edition will start on April 14th!

After the successful contest of last year we once again want to give you the opportunity to extract new meaning from the CommonCrawl webcrawl.

We provide the data. We provide the data-processing infrastructure. Can you find what new insight is hiding in these 2.8 billion web pages?

The contest will run from April 14 2014 to June 30 2014 and is open for teams of up to three members. The contest is open to all, but only students and researchers from universities, 'hogescholen' and research institutes in the Benelux are eligible to win a prize. You don't have to start at April 14! You can join at any moment, as long as you submit your results before the deadline.

We are still updating this website as new information about the 2014 edition becomes available.
