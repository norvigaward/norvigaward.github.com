Can I apply for this award?

Are you a student or researchers studying at or employed by a research institute or university in The Netherlands? Then you're probably allowed to enter.

What should I do?

Run experiments on the Common Crawl dataset using Sara's Hadoop infrastructure, and show what is innovative about those experiments.

What can I win?

The winner gets a tablet (type tba), and 1500 Euro to spend on travel, accommodation, and conference ticket for SIGIR 2013, for one person, to be held in Dublin, Ireland.

Who's on the jury?

Peter Norvig, Hilary Mason, and Evert Lammerts. Who they are? Find out here.
