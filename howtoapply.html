How can you apply?

You can apply if you are a student at a university in The Netherlands, or a researcher at a public institution in The Netherlands.

Important note: We allow a limited amount of applicants, so we can guarantee availability of the data and computing resources. We also reserve the right to deny access to all applicants at any stage in the process.

Send an email (using your institute email address) to hadoop.support@sara.nl, and include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your affiliation (institute, department, group)
  • Your position
  • Your telephone number
  • Your github username (see why at Submit Results)

If you apply with more than one person, you need to send these details for each member of your team and point out who the main applicant is. Note that the prize is awarded only to the main applicant.

If your application is approved we will send you instructions on how to proceed. If your application is denied for whatever reason, we will let you know as well.
